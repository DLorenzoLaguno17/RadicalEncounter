# Zuateke

## Idea
You are the ruler of a zombie night club, and you have to make the local grow.

## Mechanics
In order to improve your disco, you can spend money on:
* Drinks.
* Security.
* Furniture.
* Music Bands/DJ.
* Tickets.
* Cleaning staff.
* Speakers.
* Dress code.

There will be different stats such as PRESTIGE, HAPPINESS, COMFORT...

There also can be different events to add variation to the game.
